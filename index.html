Chimp

Chimp is a 3D game engine with networking, lighting & shadow support, music & positional sound effects, editor, ECS supporting serialisation, and ref-counted resource management.
It was originally started as a university project and later continued as a passion project.

GitHub

Languages & Libraries: C++, OpenGL, ENet, OpenAL
